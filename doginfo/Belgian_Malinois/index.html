Originally, Belgian shepherd dogs (also called Malinois) were used to guard herds of sheep and cattle as well as to perform various jobs.
Over the years, they have become guard dogs, probing as well as detecting explosives.
In Israel, for example, they are used by the police and the army, for the purpose of guarding and locating.

Dogs of the Belgian shepherd type (Malinois), are actually an "invention" of a Belgian veterinarian named Raoul, at the end of the 18th century, who decided to invent a Belgian dog breed. The "creation" process was done in the city of Machlen, which in French is called Malin, hence the origin of its name. Apart from this type, there are two other types of Belgian Shepherds, the Grundle and Lacanois. Both are named after villages in the Brussels city area.


Characteristics of Belgian shepherd



height

The height of males is 61-66 centimeters.

The height of females is 56-61 centimeters

Weight

Males of this breed weigh 25-30 kg,

Females weigh 22-25 kg

Body structure - Belgian Malinois Shepherd is a dog with a proportional structure with upright ears and a black nose. Some would say that he has a very handsome appearance, elegant, athletic and not bulky. It is similar to the German Shepherd, but its body structure is more proportional

fur

Coat - Belgian shepherd enjoys a medium and smooth coat, but at the same time, a little rough. It includes two layers, a hard outer layer and a dense lower layer. The fur provides him with protection against various weather conditions. Something that helped him over the years when he was used as a working dog.
The coat color of a Belgian shepherd is mahogany brown, with black spots on the face and ears. Some dogs have white spots on their toes and chest.

Character traits of Belgian Malinois Shepherd dogs

Belgian shepherd is a working dog and is included in the list of the most common shepherd dogs, but at the same time it is very sensitive and loyal, very intelligent and knows how to recognize the mood of its owner. You can perform various actions with it and it does not tend to get tired easily. They have a tendency to play around and they really enjoy sports activities. It takes time for them to open up to strangers, but with time it happens and then they connect with them.


Breeding - it is important to raise the dog at home and not in the yard or kennel. They can be raised at home with children and they can also be left alone at home, while you go to work or for anything else, but it is important to take them out immediately when you get home.


health condition

A Belgian Malinois is considered a very healthy dog. However, he can deal with joint problems (especially if his parents faced a similar problem), skin allergies, as well as vision problems and epilepsy. It is rare for these type of dogs to face health problems so they tend to live longer


Grooming and hygiene - it is very important to brush them daily, since they deal with significant shedding, and the hair, ears and teeth must be cleaned often, so that dirt does not accumulate.


Nutrition - the dog must be provided with dry daily nutrition, preferably paying attention to the various ingredients, so that he can enjoy healthy and high-quality food. He should be provided with two meals a day, morning and evening.
