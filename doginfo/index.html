Welcome to Dog Information complete dog guide, the guide that gathers the really important details about well-known and common dog types in Israel.

A new dog has joined the family and you want to get to know him? The children want to adopt a puppy and you are confused about the breed? Should I be male or female? Who is suitable for a small apartment? What kind of dog is nice to children? Do you want to send your beloved dog to training and are trying to figure out what kind of student he will be? Looking for a guard dog or prefer a dog that won't bark at grandma when she comes to visit?

In our complete dog guide you can find all the information you ever wanted to know about a selection of dog types, so you can get to know your favorite dogs better. Each page in the guide is dedicated to a different breed and will tell you in a convenient and readable way about the breed's unique characteristics, its character, typical health problems, its training, its ideal breeding place and its suitability for family and children.
