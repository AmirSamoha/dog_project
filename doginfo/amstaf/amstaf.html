Amstaff dogs (American Staffordshire Terrier) have a necessarily unfavorable image. In the eyes of many, this is a violent and aggressive dog. But this is not necessarily so, since he has many positive qualities that make him a good, loyal dog, who serves him and can be his best friend, if you know how to approach him and take care of him properly and give him a lot of love.

Amstaff is a pairing of a bulldog with other terriers. His name is the initials of AM - American, and Staff - Staffordshire (American Terrier). It was originally bred for dog fighting. In the 19th century they began to use it for guarding and hunting. In the 20th century, he was converted to serve as a companion and show dog. Over the years, they stopped using it for dog fights and ruled them illegal.

Character traits of Amstaff dogs

As we mentioned, Amstaff has the image of an aggressive and violent dog. This is why it is also classified by the Israeli government (as well as many other governments in the world) as a dangerous dog, and anyone who owns an American Amstaff must spay or neuter the dog in order to prevent reproduction.


At the same time, it is important to emphasize, his image often stems from incorrect training and use for illegal purposes. Whoever trains, takes care of and raises American Staffordshire Terrier dogs, i.e. American Amstaff, correctly, will get to enjoy a very good and well-loved dog.


It is true that the Amstaff dog has the qualities of a stubborn, determined and alert dog, but he is also very loyal to his owner, curious and smart, and he also has a lot of patience. That's why he also gets along very well with other pets as well as people, once he feels comfortable with them


An American Amstaff dog, very loyal to his owner, he does not feel the need to be dominant at home and gets along great with children. At the same time, because he is considered a dangerous dog according to the Israeli Dog Law, it is better not to raise him in apartments but in houses with a yard or garden. There he can also express his abilities as a guard dog.

Physical activity - Amstaff dogs need daily activity of 30-40 minutes a day. These are energetic dogs that need to be taken outside, allowed to run amok and have fun. What's more, these are curious and smart dogs, so outdoor activities can also help them satisfy their curiosity.


Amstaff training

The stage of training is very important for Amstaff dogs. Untrained dogs can be aggressive, to such a degree that they may bite people and other dogs. In order to eradicate their sense of aggression and create a proper socialization process for them, it is important to take them to a professional trainer at a young age.
Among other things, he will teach them how to suppress the desire for territoriality, the desire to attack as well as the desire to escape.
Amstaff dogs learn the training process very quickly, as they want to please their owner and it is important for them to obey him. The training will be done while they are tied to the leash all the time.
