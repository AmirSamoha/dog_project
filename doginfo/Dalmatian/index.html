The history of the Dalmatian dog

Dalmatian dogs are an ancient breed, which have changed throughout history. According to the information, these dogs have existed for more than two thousand years and the exact origin of these dogs is from Yugoslavia, but many researchers believe that the breed in general was developed in England from a cross between a pointer dog and a bull terrier.


Although this is an intelligent, dominant, curious and stubborn dog, his great vigor and natural hunting instinct is inherent in him, he may still destroy furniture in the house and even chase cats, but due to his hunting instinct and his high intelligence, he is easy to train and therefore, it is recommended to train him from the age of 9 months . When you start training him at the right age, you can achieve impressive results and he can become a very disciplined and loyal dog who knows his place in front of the members of the household.
