Boxer is a dog that is considered a large breed with a wide body and a muscular and massive structure.
The temperament of a boxer dog is known for its captivating character and also its temperament is very easy going.
It is a loyal breed of dog, it is brave, alert and alert, playful and full of energy. Learn all about the boxer dog in the following article:

This dog, known for its high intelligence, is sociable and also comfortable with children's weather. Sometimes he may be aggressive to strangers. The Boxer is a dog that originates from Germany. The shades of the Boxer type dog are: yellow, white, red and brown.
It should be known that Boxer dogs are considered dogs whose puppyhood period is the longest and the meaning is that their maturation is very slow. The long puppy periods have advantages and disadvantages. During this period, the dog is full of a lot of energy, the dog will be interested in playing, running, frolicking and has a need for mental and physical stimulation on a regular basis. The training will be done at a relatively late age compared to other dog breeds.


Characteristics of Boxer



Boxer in the performance of duties such as army and police

It is known that both the army and the police use the Boxer breed in all kinds of roles such as:
reconnaissance and rescue.
This dog is a perfect companion, an excellent guard dog, which gives it suitable qualities for the use of these dogs for the purposes of the military and police forces when necessary.
It should be known that a boxer dog can live to live with the family and get used to living in an apartment as well, but on the condition that his owner takes him for a walk so that he can recover his body, walk and run. If he is in the yard, his owner must take care of a kennel that will provide him with protection from the weather, whether in the hot summer or the cold winter.
Because of the short nose of a boxer type dog, sufficient cooling of the air is not possible for him and also, the short fur of the boxer dog does not protect him from the winter cold. This is the reason, because it is recommended that during the winter days, the dog stays with his family members and also, to involve him in family activities.

History of the boxer dogs

A Boxer dog is a cross between a German Bullenbeisser, which today is already an extinct breed and is actually a descendant of the Mastiff. And the Bullenbeisser was used for hundreds of years as a hunting dog and even helped in hunting various wild animals such as bears, wild boars and even deer. The Bullenbeisser's job was to catch and hold the prey until the hunter arrived. Over the years, after regional revolutions, the Bullenbeisser breed, already stopped functioning as a hunting dog and began to help farmers in guarding and restraining the cattle.


The development of the Boxer breed began in late 1894, by Georg Alt, who was a resident of Germany from the city of Munich. Georg actually mated his female dog which was a special dog that had spotted fur and was of the Bullenweisser breed.
