Shih Tzu dogs (also known as shih tzu, dwarf shih tzu), came from China where they are considered a sacred animal. The emperor's courtiers tended to raise and nurture him. At the beginning of the twentieth century, he arrived in Europe, and in the Second World War, with the help of British soldiers, he also arrived in the United States.
Shih Tzu dogs are used as pet dogs for domestic breeding and as companion dogs for various needs.
Below is the complete guide - Shih Tzu dogs:

Weight

4-13 kg

Life Expectancy

12-15 years

Height

20-28 cm

Shih Tzus are well known for their short snout and large round eyes, as well as their ever growing coat, floppy ears, and short and stout posture. Although small in size, they are well known for their largely fun and playful personality, and calm and friendly temperament.


Characteristics of Shih Tzu



height

The dogs stand 20-28 centimeters tall.

Weight

Their weight ranges from 4 to 13 kg.

body structure The Shih Tzu is a small, compact dog, whose head and tail are carried high. Their body is slightly higher than their length, they enjoy a sturdy body. They have a rounded head, round eyes and ears covered with large amounts of hair

fur

Shih Tzu dogs enjoy a very long, double-layered fur that glides over the entire body. They shed copious amounts of hair, so it is necessary to brush their fur frequently.

The colors of the fur are white, brown, black, and sometimes red.

Shih Tzu dogs are considered hypoallergenic and especially popular for this reason

Character traits of Shih Tzu dogs

Shimo Nancy dogs are very playful and full of joy of life, friendly to the environment and especially to the members of the family that adopts them and their children. They love to play and are considered very active dogs.


At the same time, they also tend to be tough and stubborn, so it is advisable to be careful of them in different situations. These features allow them to be used as watchdogs, as they tend to bark when they are exposed to unfamiliar things and deter them.


It is important to note, these are dogs that really like attention and need activity time. Other dogs can be included in the activity, as they get along well with them. It is important to get to know him and integrate him into activities with dogs at a young age, so that he enjoys their company and above all so that he does not develop a feeling of suspicion towards them.

health condition

Shih Tzu dogs face various health problems. They tend to deal with ear infections and dental problems, so cleaning and hygiene must be taken care of. Also, because of their body structure and short legs then they tend to face spinal problems.


As we mentioned, these are playful dogs that love to play, so it's important to take them on short and long walks, give them play time, whether it's outside or at home with their own toys.

Nutrition Shih Tzu dogs are prone to obesity, so it is necessary to pay attention to regular amounts of food and make sure that they are not exceeded, so that they do not start to develop excess weight.
